# FAQs

## HOW DO I TRACK MY ONLINE ORDER?
Most orders will have a tracking link emailed to you, visit our tracking page and enter the tracking number provided.
https://auspost.com.au/mypost/track/#/search

## WILL YOU PRICE MATCH?
We will price match if the price you have seen and the competitor is in line with our price matching policy.
View our Price Matching Policy

## WHAT PAYMENT OPTIONS DO YOU OFFER?
The most popular payment options are available in-store and online.

* **PayPal**
  PayPal & PayPal Pay in 4 are available for online purchases only.
* **Credit Cards**
  Online we accept most major credit cards - Visa, MasterCard, and American Express
* **Buy Now Pay Later**
  Buy Now Pay Later options with AfterPay and Zip Pay and Zip Money
* **EFTPOS**
  Is available in-store however, not online.

View the full list here.

## WHAT DELIVERY OPTIONS DO YOU HAVE?
For selected products and Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane Metro and Regional Areas, we offer Next Day Delivery and Installation Services. To check if the product you are interested in and your postcode qualifies for these services, simply enter your postcode on the product page or checkout.
