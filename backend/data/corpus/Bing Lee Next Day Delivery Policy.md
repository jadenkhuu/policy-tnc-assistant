# Next Day Delivery

* For next day delivery, simply purchase an in-stock fridge or washer (excluding Asko, Miele, Agency and all Proforma brands) and order by 10pm Monday to Friday.
* Only available in qualifying Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane metropolitan areas (areas confirmed at Store or for Online Sales at checkout), ACT and selected regional areas where Bing Lee stores are located.
* Online purchases for next day delivery subject to phone confirmation by Bing Lee customer representative.
* Excludes purchases from eBay or any third party sites.
* Visit Delivery Services for full terms and conditions.
