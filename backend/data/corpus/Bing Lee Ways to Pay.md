# Ways to Pay

Whether you choose to shop online or in-store, Bing Lee offers a wide range of safe and secure payment and purchase options. 

## Afterpay
* Shop now, pay later.

## Zip Pay
* Own it now, pay later.

## Credit/Debit Cards
* Shop securely with ease with both Credit and Debit cards from the major providers including Visa, MasterCard and American Express.

## PayPal and PayPal Pay in 4
* Check out quickly, easily and securely online with PayPal. You can now divide your eligible purchases into 4 interest-free instalments with no late fees using PayPal Pay in 4.

## Gift Cards
* You can use both Bing Lee physical and e- gift cards online and in-store.
