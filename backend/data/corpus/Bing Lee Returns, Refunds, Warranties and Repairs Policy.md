# Returns, Refunds, Warranties and Repairs

We want you to enjoy complete satisfaction. At Bing Lee, our goal is to ensure our customers enjoy better living. This includes making sure - in the unlikely event there’s an issue with your purchase - you ultimately enjoy a great outcome.

## Australian Consumer Law

The sale of Goods by Bing Lee will be subject to certain laws including, without limitation, the Australian Consumer Law ("ACL"). Our Goods come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under this the ACL. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and for compensation for any other reasonable foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the Goods repaired or replaced if the Goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure. This policy is in addition to any rights you have at law including under the ACL which is Schedule 2 of the Competition and Consumer Act, 2010 ("Act").

## What if I change my mind on a purchase?

We understand some decisions are difficult to make!

We offer a 14 day change of mind period, but require the goods to be unopened, unused and still in a new saleable condition. If this is the case, our Customer Care team will provide a return label (for online purchases) for you to post the Goods back, or you can visit one of our stores.

If the product was purchased online, the postage fee will be deducted from any refunds.

Please note this excludes special order items. Change of mind returns may also be subject to a 20% restocking fee if the product has been opened.

## What do I need to claim a return in-store?

If you’d like to make a return in store, Bing Lee will need to check if the product is eligible for return (including the condition and age of the products). The goods will need to be:

* unmarked and complete with instruction books and accessories such as remotes, controllers, power cords, batteries, battery chargers, connectivity cables and any other out of box accessories supplied as part of the purchase; and
* accompanied with proof of purchase from Bing Lee, either by providing a receipt or other proof of transaction.

Please note that it may be necessary for the manufacturer to inspect the product in order to assess its eligibility for return. We will always do our best to acquire this assessment within a reasonable time.

While Goods are being assessed, this does not confirm that the Goods have been accepted for return.

## What do I need to claim a return online?

If you purchased Goods online via Bing Lee's website and you would like to return them, you should first contact our Customer Care team on 1300 609 228 or email customercare@binglee.com.au

Our team will ask for photos or videos of the product you’d like to return, and let you know where the Goods should be sent for our returns assessment. If you live in NSW or ACT, you may return the Goods to a Bing Lee store of your choice, just let our Customer Care team know.

## Will I be charged for redelivery?

Bing Lee will pay for redelivery of Goods if they are repaired or replaced because of a major failure, or if Bing Lee is required by law to meet the delivery cost.

## What products can’t I return?

Except where required under the ACL or other applicable consumer protection laws or regulations, the following products are not eligible for return:

* Electronic software downloads;
* Subscriptions to the software update programmes;
* Opened software where the applicable licence has been used, copied or disclosed;
* Gift Cards and recharge cards to the extent they have been used or expired.
