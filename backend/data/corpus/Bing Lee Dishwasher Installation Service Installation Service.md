# Installation Service

## DISHWASHER INSTALLATION

Your dishwasher installation service includes:

* Delivery of dishwasher
* Remove old dishwasher if needed and clean area
* Install new dishwasher and connect to existing compatible water and power connections. This may involve cutting a minimum 70mm cabinetry hole to accommodate the Anti-flood hose protector.
* Level your dishwasher.
* Run dishwasher through test cycle to make sure there are no leaks or faults.
* Show customer how to use appliance
* Remove all rubbish and clean area

Swap-overs of appliances that are like-for-like in size and requirements are included as part of every Bing Lee installation

## EXTRA WORK

If extra work needs to be done, extra charges will apply.

Extra works include:

* Adding new water outlets (To be quoted)
* Installing new power points (From $150)
* Installing complete new water lines (To be quoted)
* Installing complete new power circuits (To be quoted)
* Running of extension hoses for your dishwashers (From $80)

Bing Lee Installations will finalise the prices of any extra work for sign-off before the installation is commenced.
