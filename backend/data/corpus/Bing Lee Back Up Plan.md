# Back Up Plan

Peace of mind, guaranteed - it’s back up you can rely on.
At Bing Lee, you have the option of choosing to include a Back Up plan in your purchase.

This is a warranty and service product which covers your new purchase, including the unlikely event it suffers a mechanical or electrical failure.

## What’s included:

* Certainty as to the exact period of cover
* Experienced operational and technical staff to manage the repair and/or replacement process
* Guaranteed period for repairs 
* Availability of a loan product (subject to terms and conditions and availability)
* Toll Free technical assistance
* Freight / Delivery to and from place of repair
