# Gift Cards Terms & Conditions

The following terms apply to all Gift Cards (excluding Bonus Gift Cards):

* When ordering physical or e-Gift Cards online, a minimum amount of $10 must be loaded on each Gift Card, with a maximum of 3 Gift Cards per order.
* The total combined value of any Gift Cards in the order cannot exceed $5,000.
* Once the Gift Card is credited and in the Cardholder’s possession, the Cardholder is liable for all transactions on the Gift Card.
* Cash will not be given in exchange for any unused balance on the Gift Card, and if the purchase exceeds the available Gift Card balance, the Cardholder will be required to make up the difference with another payment method.
* Gift Cards are not redeemable for cash and cannot be reloaded.
* Gift Cards are valid for 4 years from the date of issue.

## Gift Card and e-Gift Card Delivery Methods:

* E-Gift Cards under $50 will be sent to the recipient's email address instantly. Be mindful that the recipient may need to check their "Spam" or "Junk" inbox for the e-Gift Card.
* Physical Gift Cards purchased online will be sent via Australia Post and delivery times will vary based on your location. When purchasing Gift Cards online, it is the purchaser's sole responsibility to ensure the postal address or recipient's email address is entered correctly.

Bing Lee reserves the right to change these Terms and Conditions at any time. The purchase and use of a Gift Card constitute acceptance of these terms and conditions.

For lost, stolen or damaged Bing Lee Gift Cards or if you have any reason to believe an error has occurred in relation to your Bing Lee Gift Card please contact Customer Care on 1300 609 228.
