# Store Entry Terms

## Welcome to Bing Lee!
Our store entry terms reflect our commitment to transparency, prioritising the well-being of our customers and fostering a safe, enjoyable shopping experience. Please find our Store Entry Terms here, and don’t hesitate to contact us if you have any further questions.

## ENTRY CONDITIONAL
By entering our Store, you accept and agree to observe the Store Entry Terms. If you do not accept the Store Entry Terms, we respectfully ask that you leave our Store. If, having read these Store Entry Terms you continue in Store, you will be deemed to have accepted the Store Entry Terms.

## STORE ENTRY
You agree you have entered the Store at your own risk and acknowledge that, to the maximum extent permitted by law, Bing Lee is not responsible for any loss, damage, harm or injury arising from your entry to our Store.

## WHILE IN STORE
You agree you have entered the Store at your own risk and acknowledge that, to the maximum extent permitted by law, Bing Lee is not responsible for any loss, damage, harm or injury arising from your entry to our Store. While in our Store you agree:

* to comply with any reasonable direction given by our Staff in relation to the safety of our Customers and Staff or the security of our Store;
* a. to follow instructional signage regarding entering and leaving the Store, the consumption of food and drink, hygiene, capacity limits, movement through the Store and surveillance;
* to conduct yourself in a manner which does not interfere with, disrupt or offend our Staff or other Customers;
* to make available for inspection any bags, containers, bulky clothing or equipment and any other item as reasonably requested by our Staff for inspection;
* refrain from leaving any item unattended in the Store including a bag or container;
* to keep any stroller, wheelchair or buggy with you and not obstruct the aisles of our Store;
* to ensure children under 16 years of age are accompanied by an adult and supervised at all times; and
* to wear appropriate clothing and footwear at all times.

## PROHIBITED ITEMS
Customers must not bring the following things into our Store:

* any animal other than an "assistance animal":
    * accredited under a State or Territory law to assist a person with a disability to alleviate the effects of disability;
    * accredited by an animal training organisation prescribed by the Commonwealth of Australia; or
    * trained to assist a person with a disability to alleviate the effect of that disability; and trained to meet the standards of hygiene and behaviour appropriate for an animal in a public place;
* any kind of real or imitation weapon, firearm or object that could be used as a weapon;
* any hazardous item or substance including any aerosol container, laser pointer or metal tip umbrella;
* any food or drink;
* any motor vehicle, bicycle, scooter, balance bike, tripod, selfie-stick, balloon or drone other than as required for mobility in respect of recognized disability; or
* any item which our Staff determine may cause damage to our Store or goods or otherwise conceal an object for removal.

## SMOKING
Smoking (including e-cigarettes and vaporizers) is prohibited on any Bing Lee premises including our Stores, workplaces, any area deemed to be hazardous or designated as a no smoking area.

## SAFETY AND SECURITY
Bing Lee reserves the right to:

* exclude or remove any person from the Store for any reason including breach of the Store Entry Terms;
* visually or physically inspect any bag, container or object in your possession while you are on our premises;
* refuse entry to any person who has previously refused to present a bag or container for inspection on request or who has previously been asked to leave the Store for breach of the Store Entry Terms;
* prohibit a Customer from bringing any personal item into our Store as our Staff determine; and
* operate closed circuit television (CCTV) cameras and record any activity in or about our Stores.

## SURVEILLANCE
Bing Lee may conduct camera surveillance in and around its Stores. The surveillance may be continuous and ongoing, and will be undertaken primarily for the safety and security of Staff, Customers and Bing Lee's property.

It is condition of your entry to the Store that you consent to Bing Lee conducting surveillance in Store.

Surveillance cameras may record data on storage devices for retrieval purposes when there is a security breach or a reportable incident including a theft or injury to Staff or a member of the public. A reportable incident is one that is required to be reported to Bing Lee's management or the NSW Police.

Camera surveillance in and around our Store will not be carried out unless:

* the cameras used for the surveillance (or camera casings or other equipment that would generally indicate the presence of a camera) are visible; and
* signs notifying people that they may be under surveillance are visible at each entrance to the Store.

## CHANGES TO STORE ENTRY TERMS
Bing Lee reserves the right to make changes to these terms from time to time. Any changes to these terms will be published on Bing Lee's website. Bing Lee encourages Customers and Staff to periodically review its Store Entry Terms.
